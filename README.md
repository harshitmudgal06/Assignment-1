

Q1. Explain the difference between the following data types with examples:

Ans. Integer (int): It is used for integer values. They can be positive, negative, or zero.

Example: x = 15 or y = -5

Float (float): It is used for numbers that have a decimal point or fractional part.

Example: price = 99.99 or pi = 3.14

String (str): It represents text data. It is always written inside single quotes ('...') or double quotes ("...").

Example: name = "Harshit" or age = '20'

Boolean (bool): It can only hold one of two values "True" or "False". It is mostly used for checking conditions.

Example: x = True or y = False

Q4. Explain any five commonly used string methods in Python with examples.

Ans. upper(): Converts all letters in a string to capital letters.

Example: "hello".upper() ---> "HELLO"

lower(): Converts all letters in a string to small letters.

Example: "PYTHON".lower() ---> "python"

strip(): Removes extra spaces from both the beginning and the end of a string.

Example: "  apple  ".strip() ---> "apple"

replace(): Replaces a specific part of a string with a new text.

Example: "I like tea".replace("tea", "coffee") ---> "I like coffee"

find(): Looks for a specific letter or word in a string and returns its index position. If it can't find it, it returns -1.

Example: "banana".find("n") ---> 2 (since Index starts at 0)

Q7. What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)? Explain its importance and mention any four real-life applications of AI.
What is AI?

Ans. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on building smart machines and programs that can think, learn, make decisions, and solve problems just like a human being would.

Importance of AI:
AI is highly important because it can process massive amounts of data much faster than humans. It automates repetitive tasks, reduces human errors, runs 24/7 without getting tired, and helps industries like healthcare and finance make highly accurate decisions and predictions.

Four Real-Life Applications of AI:

Healthcare: It is used for detecting diseases through medical scans and helping discover new medicines.

E-commerce: It is used by websites like Amazon to recommend products based on Intrest of User.

Social Media: It is used by apps like Instagram or YouTube to curate your feed and show videos we like.

Self Driving Vehicles: It is used in Self-Driving cars (like Tesla) to navigate roads and avoid traffic.

Q8. Identify whether the following are examples of AI and explain why:

Ans. ChatGPT:

Yes it is an AI and It uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) models to understand human text and generate smart, realistic responses on its own.

Google Maps route prediction:

Yes it is an AI and It analyzes live traffic patterns, historical data, and user speed data in real-time to predict the fastest routes.

Calculator:

No it is not an AI because It does not think or learn from data. It just follows hardcoded mathematical rules programmed into it to solve simple calculations.

Netflix recommendations:

Yes it is an AI and It uses machine learning algorithms to track our watch history and guess what movies or shows us will enjoy next.

Voice assistants (Alexa/Siri):

Yes it is an AI and Both of Them use voice recognition and AI processing to understand what we say, answer your questions, and perform tasks for us.
